Our Academic Curriculum




Pre-primary

Children at this stage learn the fundamental concepts of shapes, numbers, colours, animals, plants etc that helps ease the transition to formal schooling.

Primary

It is a framework that structures what a school sets out to teach its pupils. In this way, it covers the skills, knowledge, attitude and values, and practice.

Secondary

A broad and balanced understanding of subjects to enable students to communicate effectively, analyse information, take informed decisions, and move ahead

Sr. Secondary

The curriculum encompasses nine major learning areas, which are: Languages, Humanities, Mathematics, Science and Technology, General Studies, and Health and Physical Education.
